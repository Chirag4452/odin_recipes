Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups cooked lasagna noodles
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, flour, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the Parmesan and mozzarella cheese until smooth.
  4. Add the whisked egg mixture, Parmesan cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese mixture to the bowl with the lasagna noodles.
  5. Mix well to combine.
  6. Form the lasagna noodles into a ball, about 1 inch (2.5 cm) in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet.
